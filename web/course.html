Top Course of Nepali Universities



Science Programs

BSc. CSIT

Tribhuvan University & Pokhara University

A 4-year IT-focused course combining computer science theory with practical IT skills.

Job Skills:

  • Programming
  • Software Development
  • Database Management
  • Web Development
  • Network & Security

Course Structure (Year-wise):

  • Year 1: Fundamentals of Computer Systems, Programming (C/Java), Mathematics, Physics/Chemistry
  • Year 2: Data Structures & Algorithms, Object-Oriented Programming, Database Management Systems, Digital Logic
  • Year 3: Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Software Engineering, Web Technologies, Artificial Intelligence
  • Year 4: Advanced Programming, Electives (e.g., Mobile Computing, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security), Project Work, Internship

BE Computer

Pokhara University

Combines electronics engineering with computer software knowledge to build intelligent systems and applications.

Job Skills:

  • Embedded Systems
  • Software & Hardware Design
  • Networking
  • Robotics
  • Programming

Course Structure (Year-wise):

  • Year 1 (Sem 1 & 2): Engineering Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Programming (C/C++), Basic Electrical/Mechanical Engineering
  • Year 2 (Sem 3 & 4): Engineering Mathematics, Logic Circuits, Data Structure & Algorithm, Electronic Devices, Microprocessors, Theory of Computation
  • Year 3 (Sem 5 & 6): Numerical Methods, Microprocessor System & Interfacing, Operating System, Computer Graphics, Data Communication, Database Management System, Software Engineering, Minor Project
  • Year 4 (Sem 7 & 8): Computer Architecture, Digital Signal Processing, Computer Network, Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing & Pattern Recognition, Major Project Work, Electives

*Also available for management students with math.

Job Skills:

  • Networking
  • Web Development
  • Project Management
  • Software Engineering
  • Cybersecurity

Course Structure (Year-wise):

  • Year 1: Fundamentals of IT, Programming, Mathematics, Internet Technology
  • Year 2: Object-Oriented Programming, Data Structures, Database Management, Digital Systems
  • Year 3: Software Engineering, Computer Architecture, Operating Systems, Data Communication & Networks
  • Year 4: Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Digital Marketing, Research Methods, Project Work, Specializations

Job Skills:

  • Architectural Design
  • Urban Planning
  • Building Construction

  • Sustainable Design

Course Structure (Year-wise):

Entrance Preparation Support

Before starting your academic journey, many courses require you to pass an entrance exam. We are here to guide and support you in cracking them successfully!

We help you prepare for:

  • KUMAT
    For KU Business & IT programs
  • PU Entrance
    For PU-based courses like BCA, BCSIT, BDevS, BBA etc.
  • Aptitude Test
    For BDes, BArch (Design and Architecture courses)

We help you prepare for:

  • IOE Entrance
    For BE Computer (Tribhuvan University, Engineering)
  • IOST Entrance
    For BSC CSIT (Science and IT programs under TU)
  • CMAT
    For BBA, BIM, BBM, BHM, BCIS (Management and IT under TU/PU)

Join our dedicated entrance preparation modules, mock tests, and expert mentoring to increase your success rate.
