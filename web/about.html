About StudyNep

StudyNP is your trusted guide to higher education opportunities in Nepal. Whether you're planning to study at a national university, a foreign-affiliated college, or looking for the right scholarship or course, we are here to support your journey every step of the way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the process of choosing the right academic path by providing accurate, updated, and practical information about universities, programs, and scholarships. We aim to empower students to make informed decisions that align with their career goals and interests.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our actions.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do.
  • Empowerment: We empower students to take charge of their educational journeys.

Why Choose Us?

  • We provide a comprehensive list of universities and colleges to help students find the right fit.
  • Expert advice on course selection tailored to each student's interests and goals.
  • Updated information on courses and scholarships to keep students informed.
  • Personalized guidance through mentoring sessions as students strive for success.
  • Friendly, student-focused support at every step of your academic journey.
  • Preparation for entrance exams to boost your confidence and readiness.
  • Skill development training relevant to your chosen course.
  • Assistance with admission processes to your desired college.
  • Guidance and support in securing scholarships.
  • Helping you become skillful and career-ready as you strive for your goals.

To ensure you get the personal support you need, we offer one-on-one consultations. Our experienced mentors help you understand your options, clarify your goals, and take the right steps toward your future.
